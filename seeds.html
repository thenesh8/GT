Guar Seed

Harmonized System (HS) code for "Guar Seed" is 130232
Botanical Name: Cyamopsis tetragonoloba
Guar Seed, the botanical marvel known as Cyamopsis tetragonoloba, is the source of one of the most versatile and sought-after natural products, guar gum. This hardy plant, native to India and Pakistan, produces seeds that have garnered immense attention for their multifaceted industrial applications.
Specifications
  • Seed Color: Typically brown to green, depending on the variety.
  • Seed Shape: Spherical or oval.
  • Seed Size: Approximately 5-8 mm in diameter
  • Seed Weight: Varies by variety and growing conditions
  • Moisture Content: Typically less than 12%
  • Purity: Typically more than 98%
  • Germination Rate: Typically more than 80%
  • Foreign Matter Content: Typically less than 2%
  • Admixture Content: Typically less than 2%
  • Packaging: Typically available in bulk sacks or bags as per customer requirements.

At GTE, our commitment to excellence shines through our sourcing of premium Guar Seeds from trusted suppliers. Our Guar Seeds undergo rigorous quality assessments to ensure their purity and adherence to industry standards, assuring you of a top-tier raw material for your business.

Key Features
Versatility at its Core

Guar Seeds serve as the foundation for guar gum, a remarkable substance celebrated for its outstanding thickening, stabilizing, and emulsifying properties across various industries.

Eco-Friendly Champion

Guar is recognized for its eco-friendly characteristics. It thrives in arid conditions, helping conserve water, and its nitrogen-fixing abilities make it a responsible choice for sustainable agriculture.

Food-Grade

Our Guar Gum Split is Suiteable for food applications, where it acts as a valuable additive in a wide range of products, from sauces and dressings to ice cream and gluten-free baked goods.

Agricultural Significance

Guar Seeds have significant agricultural implications, benefiting both farmers and the environment. Their role as a nitrogen-fixing crop enhances soil fertility and supports sustainable farming practices.

Industrial Ubiquity

These seeds are of paramount importance in numerous industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, textiles, and oil and gas. Guar gum, derived from Guar Seeds, plays an indispensable role as a vital additive, thickener, and stabilizer.

Incorporate Guar Seeds from GTE into your processes and unlock the potential of this versatile and high-quality natural resource. Whether you operate in the food, pharmaceutical, textile, or any other industry, Guar Seeds are a reliable foundation for your diverse needs.
Packaging Choices
Packaging choices for Guar Gum Powder can vary depending on your specific requirements and preferences. It primarly depends on the factors such as the quantity , transportation and storage conditions, and any specific requirements. Here are some common packaging options for Guar Seed:

Multi-Ply Paper Bags

Guar Gum Powder is often packaged in multi-ply paper bags, typically available in various sizes such as 25 kg, 50 kg, or custom sizes. These bags are sturdy and provide protection from moisture and contamination.

Polypropylene Bags

Polypropylene bags are another popular choice for packaging Guar Gum Powder. These bags offer excellent moisture resistance and durability, making them Suiteable for both domestic and international shipping.

Fiber Drums

Guar Gum Powder can also be packaged in fiber drums, especially for larger quantities. Fiber drums are sturdy, stackable, and provide good protection from external factors.

Kraft Bags with Inner Liner

Some suppliers offer Guar Gum Powder in kraft paper bags with an inner polyethylene liner. This combination provides extra protection against moisture and ensures product integrity.

Bulk Packaging

For industrial or bulk applications, Guar Gum Powder may be available in bulk packaging options, such as supersacks or flexible intermediate bulk containers (FIBCs).

Custom Packaging

Depending on your specific needs and branding requirements, some suppliers may offer custom packaging solutions, including branded bags, labels, or packaging with your desired specifications.

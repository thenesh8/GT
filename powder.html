Guar Gum Powder

Harmonized System (HS) code for "Guar Seed" is 130-232-30
Botanical Name: Cyamopsis Tetragonoloba
Guar Gum Powder, a versatile natural substance derived from guar beans, is celebrated for its remarkable thickening, stabilizing, and emulsifying properties. At GTE, we take pride in offering premium Guar Gum Powder sourced from trusted suppliers. This essential product has found widespread use in various industries, making it a go-to solution for enhancing product quality.
Specifications

Guar Gum Powder is a natural hydrocolloid obtained from the endosperm of guar seeds (Cyamopsis tetragonoloba). It is widely recognized for its versatile thickening, stabilizing, and gelling properties, making it a valuable ingredient in various industries.

    Physical Characteristics
  • Appearance: Fine, pale off-white to yellowish powder
  • Odor: Odorless or with a faint characteristic odor
  • Taste: Tasteless
    Particle Size
  • Mesh Size: Typically 200 mesh (74 microns) and higher
    Chemical Characteristics
  • Viscosity (2% Solution): Typically 2,000 to 7,000 cps (Brookfield RVT, spindle 3, 20 RPM at 25°C)
  • Moisture Content: Maximum 12%
  • Ash Content: Maximum 1.5%
  • pH: 5.5 to 7.0 (1% solution)
  • Protein Content: Maximum 5%
  • Insoluble Residue: Maximum 3%
    Microbiological Parameters

    VGuar Gum Powder typically conforms to industry standards for microbial specifications.

    Chemical Constituents

    Guar Gum Powder mainly comprises galactomannan, which is a high-molecular-weight carbohydrate composed of mannose and galactose units.

    Packaging

    Guar Gum Powder is commonly available in multi-layer paper bags, polyethylene-lined bags, or fiber drums. Packaging sizes typically include 25 kg bags, 50 lb bags, or as per customer requirements.

    Storage

    Store Guar Gum Powder in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and extreme temperature fluctuations.

Quality and Purity

Key Features
Exceptional Thickening and Stabilizing Agent

Guar Gum Powder is celebrated for its remarkable thickening, emulsifying, and stabilizing properties. It enhances the texture, shelf life, and overall quality of a wide array of products.

Food-Grade Excellence

Our Guar Gum Powder is ideal for food applications, where it serves as a vital additive in various products, including baked goods, sauces, dairy items, enhance texture, and gluten-free formulations.

Pharmaceutical and Cosmetic Applications

Guar Gum Powder is an essential component in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries, where it acts as a binder, disintegrating agent, and thickening agent in tablet formulations, lotions, and creams.

Oil and Gas Sector

In the oil and gas industry, Guar Gum Powder is used as a critical component for drilling and hydraulic fracturing processes, where it serves as a shale stabilizer and viscosifier.

Natural and Sustainable

Derived from guar beans, Guar Gum Powder is an environmentally responsible choice. The guar plant's drought resistance and nitrogen-fixing properties make it an eco-friendly resource.

Guar Gum Powder's versatility extends to a wide range of industries, including textiles, paper, mining, and more, where it plays an integral role in creating stable emulsions, controlling viscosity, and improving product texture.
